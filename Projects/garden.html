Process & Concept

The Garden: Flowers & Fireworks project began with the idea of creating an interactive space where users could engage with nature in a digital format.
Inspired by a flower pattern generator , I expanded the concept to generate fireworks as an interactive element. Using p5.js, I designed a system that allows users to plant customizable virtual flowers or fireworks thorugh mouse clicks. The project explores themes of interaction beauty, and celebration.
Through iterative design and user feedback, I refined the interactivity and visual aesthetics to include a customizable UI controls and four seasonal themes.
